About Me

My name is Natalia, and I am currently studying to become a web developer. Transitioning to this new profession is a significant step for me, and I hope that I will find it fulfilling and that it will allow me to realize my potential.

Previously, I worked as a production planning engineer at a factory specializing in the manufacture of bulletproof vests, bulletproof glass, and other protective products. This experience taught me to value precision, discipline, and responsibility in my work.

In my free time, I love to draw and create order out of chaos. Art is a way for me to express my thoughts and feelings, while my passion for order reflects my appreciation for organization and a structured approach to tasks.

I enjoy watching TV series and movies as a way to relax and immerse myself in different worlds and stories. Additionally, the sea, the sun, and traveling are sources of energy and inspiration for me. I love exploring new countries, cities, and mountains, learning about different cultures, and collecting unforgettable experiences.

My goal is to successfully develop in the field of web development. I am confident that acquiring new knowledge and skills will allow me to excel in this profession and find my place in the IT world.
